About Us

Welcome to QDRC Physiotherapy, where we are dedicated to helping you achieve optimal physical health and wellness. Our experienced team of physiotherapists uses evidence-based treatments to help you recover from injuries, manage chronic conditions, and improve your overall quality of life.

With state-of-the-art facilities and personalized care plans, we ensure that each patient receives the attention and treatment they deserve.
